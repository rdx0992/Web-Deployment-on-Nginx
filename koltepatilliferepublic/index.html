Why Hinjawadi? Explore the Benefits

Located in north-western outskirts of Pune, Hinjawadi is a prominent employment hub situated 19 km from the city center. It has also emerged as a residential locality comprising large township projects.

The presence of various prominent schools, colleges, hospitals and shopping malls has prompted many home buyers to invest their money in this area.

The locality is placed at Mumbai Highway (NH-48) and is closely connected to Wakad, Baner, Tathawade and Balewadi. It majorly hosts a mix of completed as well as under-construction multi-storey apartment projects.

About Kolte Patil Life Republic Universe

Kolte-Patil Developers is a top real estate corporation with a prevailing presence in the Pune residential market. It was founded around 3 decades ago and is driven by a straightforward yet profound philosophy of ‘Creation, not construction.

The company was incorporated in 1991, is headquartered in Pune, and is listed on NSE and BSE. Kolte-Patil Developers has been building landmarks and has constructed over 50 projects including commercial complexes, residential complexes, and IT Parks encircling a saleable area of ~20 million sq. ft. across Pune, Mumbai, and Bangalore.

It is a trusted name and has a well-established reputation for providing high-quality standards, originality, and transparency. The Company’s long-term bank debt and non-convertible debentures have been rated ‘STABLE’ by CRISIL
